tools: [channels, workflows]

1. Read the channel, referenced workflow, and existing routing configuration first.
2. Preserve recipient, suppression, approval, and rate-limit safeguards.
3. Validate every referenced workflow and route before proposing the complete CHANNEL.md through `channels`.
4. Tell the owner the change requires Approval Inbox review and may require a service restart to activate transport wiring.
